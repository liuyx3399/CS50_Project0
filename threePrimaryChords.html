So we have the C major tonic triad, built on the tonic of the scale. The subdominant triad is built on the fourth note, and the dominant triad on the fifth. The roman numerals written underneath refer to these scale degrees and are another important means of identifying the different triads in the scale. We will use this much more in the coming lectures.
